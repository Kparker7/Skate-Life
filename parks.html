Chicago's Best Skateparks

Chicago has a strong skating scene with a mix of public and DIY skateparks. Here are some top picks:

  • Grant Park Skatepark – Downtown Chicago
    Large concrete park with bowls, rails, ledges, and skyline views. Great for all skill levels.
  • Wilson Skatepark – Uptown
    Well maintained park with mini ramps, quarter pipes, and street style obstacles near the lake.
  • Logan Boulevard Skate Spot – Logan Square
    Popular DIY style skate spot under the bridge. Bring wax!
  • Burnside Skatepark (Chicago) – South Side
    This smaller park has bowls and urban vibes.
  • Audubon Skate Spot – Roscoe Village
    Small neighborhood setup with rails and ledges. Great for beginners and casual sessions.

Plan Your Visit

Before heading out, make sure to check the park's hours, bring your gear, and follow posted rules. Chicago parks are busiest after school and on weekends.
